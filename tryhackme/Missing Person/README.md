**Question 1: What is the commercial name of this circuit?**

It is seen on the image that the circuit has a huge "PERTAMINA" statue and the image name is MotoGP. So the first thing that came to mind is a MotoGP circuit in Indonesia. After looking it up, I found that the circuit is Pertamina Mandalika International Street Circuit which held a very popular MotoGP event back in 2025.

Answer: Pertamina Mandalika International Street Circuit

**Question 2: When did the event take place?**

After looking up "MotoGP Mandalika Date 2025" on the internet, I found that the event took place on 3 to 5th of October 2025

Answer: 03-05/10/2025

**Question 3: He told me he ate delicious Mexican food. What is the name of the restaurant?**

Using Google Lens, I used the image given and inserted it into the field and the first image shown gives out the name of the place.
